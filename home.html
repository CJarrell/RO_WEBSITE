Services

Whatever is needed to help you achieve a calmer, more pleasurable existence, R&O can help you realize it.

Personal Home Organizing

Everybody has an organizing project they’ve been putting off for far too long. It’s nothing to be ashamed of! R&O is here maximize your quality of life by completing that project for you. Regardless of whether it's the smallest closet in your house, or the whole house. We want to transform your space into the personal sanctuary you deserve. We wanna help you face your space, tell us how below!

Life Management Consulting

Organizing is a skill. It takes time, patience, and dedication to develop the habits needed to be consistently organized. As the lead organizer at R&O, I respect the ol'sang about “teaching a person to fish”. Let me teach you the organizing skills that built me a successful business. After a tour of your priority areas, I'll map out a strategy for you to get organized. On your own terms and time frame.

Full Moves / Packing / Unpacking

Moving is the MOST stressful event in life. Let R&O be your worry stone, facilitating every aspect of your move. From helping you pack and stay on track leading up to your moving day. To unpacking every box so you can feel at home in your brand new space. We’ll even help you find the perfect moving company that offers coverage and care. Teleportation is real and we have the technology!
