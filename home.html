Services

R&O offers a variety of services to help you achieve a calmer, simpler, more pleasurable life.

Personal Home Organizing

Everybody has an organizing project they’ve been putting off for far too long. It’s nothing to be ashamed of! R&O is here maximize your quality of life by completing that project for you. Regardless of whether it's the smallest closet in your house, or the whole house. We want to transform your space into the personal sanctuary you deserve. We wanna help you face your space, tell us how below!

Life Management Consulting

Organizing is a skill. It takes time, patience, and dedication to develop the habits needed to be consistently organized. As the lead organizer at R&O, I respect the ol'sang about “teaching a person to fish”. Let me teach you the organizing skills that built me a successful business. After a tour of your priority areas, I'll map out a strategy for you to get organized. On your own terms and time frame.

Full Moves / Packing / Unpacking

Moving is the MOST stressful event in life. Let R&O be your worry stone, facilitating every aspect of your move. From helping you pack and stay on track leading up to your moving day. To unpacking every box so you can feel at home in your brand new space. We’ll even help you find the perfect moving company that offers coverage and care. Teleportation is real and we have the technology!

OUR APPROACH

#1

Spatial Assessment

Don’t clean up prior to the initial visit; it is much more helpful to see your home in all its natural glory! Your organizer will walk through your home with you and encourage you to talk freely about what the space is not providing, what is bothering you, what drives you crazy, and what helps you stay organized. After this assessment, your Organizer will prepare a proposal and email it to you within a couple days for approval.

Sort & Categorize

You and your organizer will empty out the spaces you have prioritized. For every item that comes out, it will be given a category: Keep (including items that should change rooms), Toss (trash & recycling), Donations, or Pending. These categories usually expand and evolve as the organizing process continues. If your anxious about making the tough decisions, don't worry, that's part of our job: to provide emotional support. Once each item is with its friends, we are ready to Rise & Organize!

#3

Strategize & Organize

Before putting items back (in an orderly fashion!), you and your Organizer will talk about how the space will be used, what the functionality of the area will be, and how you - as an individual - operate on a day to day basis. This step is crucial in creating a space that will be functional for you and that you will be able to maintain with as little effort as possible. There may need to be a shopping list if there are tools or containers that are necessary for completing the project.

Donate & Consign

Your Organizer will take as many donations/consignments that she can fit in her car at the end of each session. For larger items and bigger projects, we will happily arrange for a donations pick up and/or junk removal. R&O works with a handful of local consignment shops and will transport the items.

About

A brief history of our RISE in the organizing industry

One day while I was organizing my home office, filing papers and categorizing my junk drawers, I had a realization: while most people find organizing tedious, I find the activity enjoyable and gratifying. At first, this ephiphany made me feel a bit like a weirdo. Now I see I was using a skill that I could develop into a new career. When people say to "do what you love", most tend to overlook the skills they naturally use on a day-to-day basis, such as being organized. When it comes down to it, I am grateful I have found a way to make a living doing something I love. The best part is I've found a job that provides oppurtunities to help people.

In addition to the organizing work I do, I also volunteer my time and home to help manage the homeless cat population. Please get your pets spayed or neutered! I am happy to provide you low-cost resources so you can help in this effort too. Feel free to contact me below!
